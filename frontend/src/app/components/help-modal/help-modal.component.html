HOW TO PLAY

Check out the matchup
Two cars are lined up for the daily race. Each car has key stats like Engine, Power, Weight, and Drivetrain to help you decide.
Make your pick
Which car will win? Select the car you think is the fastest.
Watch the race
After picking, the race video will unlock.
Watch as the cars battle it out!
See Results
Did you guess correctly? Compare your pick with the actual winner and view how other players chose!
